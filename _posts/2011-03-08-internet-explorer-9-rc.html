The RC of Internet Explorer 9 is out at the following address: http://windows.microsoft.com/ie9

For the moment, I’m exclusively using Chrome for browsing. I started using it when it first came out to give it a try, and it was so amazingly fast that returning to any other browser was painful. However, with this new version of IE, things have changed. IE9 is very fast. Also, the search function works like in Chrome, which is obviously a plus.

So, what I will do is try to use IE9 exclusively for 30 days and see what comes out of it. If I enjoy it enough, I might switch to it as my main browser, who knows?

For now, the two things I want from Chrome is offline mails for gmail (which might come with Gears that I’m installing right now) and a spell checker.
